PAW CONTROL (FREE)

  • Filter MIDI notes
  • Randomize MIDI notes relative to your input
  • Route to external instruments or use internal sine synth
  • Optimized for both live performance and studio
  • Endless fun for you and your friends, be it with hands or paws!

Paw Control is a VST instrument that lets you filter MIDI notes in new and creative ways. With features for randomizing notes relative to YOUR input in real-time, it plays along with your creative vision while slightly nudging you into new realms of musical discovery. The purpose of this instrument is to enhance your creativity rather than trying to replace it. Paw Control also includes a simple sine synth and is super easy to route forward to any MIDI instrument, be it software or hardware.
