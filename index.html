Innovative Plugins for Creative Minds

Welcome to Floof Audio, where imagination meets innovation in the realm of sound. Born from a passion project in the vibrant city of Oslo, Norway, on a journey to transform the way artists create. Drawing inspiration from the ethereal qualities of sound and the boundless possibilities of technology, Floof Audio offers a unique collection of VST/AAX/AU plugins. Each creation is meticulously crafted to inspire musicians, producers, and sound engineers around the globe to explore new auditory landscapes. Join us in our mission to push the boundaries of digital sound and discover the undiscovered with Floof Audio.
